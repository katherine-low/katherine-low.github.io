Top 3 Dishes in South Korea

Naengmyeon

Naengmyeon is a South Korean dish that consists of cold buckwheat noodles in a chilled meat or kimchi broth. The cold soup provides a refreshing meal to cool off from the heat, so it is typically eaten in the summer.
